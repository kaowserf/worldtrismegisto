Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping within the US typically takes 3-5 business days. International shipping can take 7-14 business days depending on the destination. Express shipping options are available at checkout for faster delivery.

Are your tarot readings accurate?

Our tarot readings are personalized based on ancient mystical traditions and are designed to provide spiritual guidance. While they offer insights and perspectives, they should be viewed as tools for self-reflection rather than definitive predictions.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for unworn, unwashed items in their original packaging. Please note that customized items cannot be returned unless there's a manufacturing defect. Visit our Shipping & Returns page for more details.

Do you offer custom designs?

Yes, we offer custom designs for special orders and collaborations. Please contact us directly with your requirements, and our design team will work with you to create a unique piece that resonates with your spiritual vision.

Are your products ethically made?

Absolutely. We are committed to ethical manufacturing practices. All our products are made in facilities that ensure fair wages and safe working conditions. We also use eco-friendly materials wherever possible to minimize our environmental impact.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International customers may be responsible for customs fees and import duties in accordance with their country's regulations. Please check our Shipping Information page for the complete list of countries we serve.
