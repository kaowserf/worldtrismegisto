Our Story

Discover the mystical journey behind World Trismegisto

The Genesis

Founded in 2018, World Trismegisto was born from a profound appreciation for ancient wisdom and mystical traditions.

Our journey began when our founder, Elena Morin, discovered her grandmother's collection of tarot cards and mystical artifacts hidden in an old cedar chest. These treasures, passed down through generations, sparked a lifelong fascination with the esoteric arts.

After years of studying ancient symbolism, sacred geometry, and the mystical traditions of various cultures, Elena decided to create a brand that would bring these powerful symbols and their meanings into contemporary fashion.

The name "World Trismegisto" pays homage to Hermes Trismegistus, the purported author of the Hermetic Corpus, a series of sacred texts that form the basis of Hermeticism. These teachings emphasize the connection between the physical and spiritual realms - a philosophy that permeates every aspect of our designs.

Ethical Production

Our commitment to sustainability and ethical production is as integral to our brand as our mystical aesthetics.

We believe that truly conscious fashion must honor not only ancient wisdom but also our planet and its people. That's why we've implemented rigorous standards for our production process:

  • ✧ We use organic cotton and recycled materials wherever possible
  • ✧ Our dyes are low-impact and often plant-based
  • ✧ All workers receive fair wages and safe working conditions
  • ✧ We minimize waste through careful planning and recycling programs
  • ✧ Our packaging is plastic-free and made from recycled materials

We regularly visit our production facilities to ensure our high standards are maintained, and we're constantly seeking ways to improve our environmental footprint.
